Job Description - IT Support Technician

Position Overview

  • Reference Number: IT7A3S
  • Position Title: IT Support Technician
  • Salary Range: 15,000 - 20,000 QAR per month
  • Location: Doha, Qatar
  • Job Type: Full-Time
  • Reports To: Senior IT Administrator

Brief Description of the Position

The IT Support Technician provides technical support, analyzes hardware and software issues, ensures the smooth operation of IT systems, maintains company networks, and supports end-users while contributing to IT service improvements.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Ensure compliance with IT security policies and data protection standards.
  2. Keep detailed records of issues encountered and solutions provided.
  3. Provide technical support to end-users, ensuring timely resolution of incidents.
  4. Maintain and monitor IT infrastructure including servers, networks, and cloud services.
  5. Assist in implementing and managing cloud-based solutions.
  6. Collaborate with IT team members on projects and upgrades.
  7. Communicate with customers to provide technical solutions.

Required Qualifications, Skills & Attributes

  • Essential:
  • Bachelor's degree in IT, Computer Science, or relevant field.
  • 3+ years experience in IT development.
  • Proficiency in HTML, Python, and JavaScript.
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
  • Knowledge of hardware, software, and networking.
  • Ability to perform routine maintenance.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Experience with Microsoft 365 and user support.
  • Preferable:
  • Certifications: CompTIA A+, Network+, Microsoft Azure Fundamentals.
  • Experience with virtualization: VMware, Hyper-V.
  • Experience with ticketing systems: Jira, ServiceNow, Zendesk.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud.
  • Fluency in Arabic.

Compensation & Benefits

  • Competitive salary package
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Flexible working hours
  • Annual performance bonuses
  • Housing allowance

Job Description - Project Manager

Position Overview

  • Reference Number: PM7H5Q
  • Position Title: Project Manager
  • Salary Range: 18,000 - 24,000 QAR per month
  • Location: Doha, Qatar
  • Job Type: Full-Time
  • Reports To: Chief Technology Officer

Brief Description of the Position

The Project Manager is responsible for planning, executing, and closing projects according to deadlines and budget. This includes coordinating resources, team members, and third-party contractors to deliver projects successfully.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Define project scope, goals, and deliverables.
  2. Develop project plans and communication documents.
  3. Estimate resources and participants needed.
  4. Identify and manage project dependencies and critical paths.
  5. Plan and schedule project timelines and milestones.
  6. Track milestones and deliverables.
  7. Prepare reports, proposals, and presentations.

Required Qualifications, Skills & Attributes

  • Essential:
  • Bachelor's in Business Admin, Management, or related field.
  • 5+ years experience in project management.
  • Experience with project management software and methodologies.
  • Leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills.
  • Strong organization and multitasking abilities.
  • PMP or PRINCE2 certification is a plus.
  • Preferable:
  • Experience in IT industry.
  • Familiarity with Agile and Scrum.
  • Fluency in Arabic.

Compensation & Benefits

  • Competitive salary package
  • Paid time off
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Flexible working hours
  • Annual performance bonuses
  • Housing allowance
