Job Description - IT Support Technician

Position Overview

  • Reference Number: IT7A3S
  • Position Title: IT Support Technician
  • Salary Range: 15,000-20,000 QAR per month
  • Location: Doha, Qatar
  • Job Type:Full-Time
  • Reports To: Senior IT Administrator

Brief Description of the Position

The IT Support Technician provides techinical support, Analyzing hardware and software issues, and ensuring the smooth operation of IT systems within the organization, maintain company networks and ensure smooth performance of computer systems. This role involves supporting end-users and contributing to IT service improvements.

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Ensure compliance with IT security policies, data protection standards, and best practices across all user environments.
  2. Keep detailed records of issues encountered and solution provided.This documentation helps in resolving future problems more efficiently.
  3. Provide technical support to end-users across the organisation, ensuring timely resolution of incidents and service requests.
  4. Maintain and monitor IT infrastructure, including servers, networks, and cloud services.
  5. Assist in the implementation and management of cloud-based solutions and services.
  6. Collaborate with other IT team members to plan and execute IT projects and upgrades.
  7. Talk to customers over the phone or via email to provide solutions to technical problems

Required Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge & Attributes:

    Essential

  • Bachelor's degree in Information Technology,Computer Science or relevant field.
  • 3+ years of experience in It development
  • Required programming language:HTML, Python and Javascript
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
  • Strong knowledge of computer hardware, software, and networking.
  • perform routine maintenance of computer/network systems.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Experience with Microsoft 365 and user support

    • Preferable

  • Certifications such as CompTIA A+, Network+, or Microsoft Certified: Azure Fundamentals.
  • Experience with virtualization technologies (e.g., VMware, Hyper-V).
  • Experience with ticketing systems such as Jira, ServiceNow, or Zendesk.
  • Familiarity with cloud computing platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).
  • Fluency in Arabic

Compensation & Benefits

  • Competitive salary package
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Flexible working hours
  • Annual performance bonuses
  • Housing Allowance

Job Description - Project Manager

Position Overview

  • Reference Number: PM7H5Q
  • Position Title: Project Manager
  • Salary Range: 18,000-24,000 QAR per month
  • Location: Doha, Qatar
  • Job Type:Full-Time
  • Reports To: Chief Technology Officer

Brief Description of the Position

The Project Manager is responsible for planning, executing, and closing projects according to deadlines and within budget. This includes acquiring resources and coordinating the efforts of team members and third-party contractors or consultants in order to deliver projects according to plan. The Project Manager will also define the project's objectives and oversee quality control throughout its life cycle.

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Define project scope, goals, and deliverables that support business goals in collaboration with senior management and stakeholders.
  2. Develop full-scale project plans and associated communications documents.
  3. Estimate the resources and participants needed to achieve project goals.
  4. Identify and manage project dependencies and critical path.
  5. Plan and schedule project timelines and milestones using appropriate tools.
  6. Track project milestones and deliverables.
  7. Develop and deliver progress reports, proposals, requirements documentation, and presentations.

Required Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge & Attributes:

    Essential

  • Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Management, or a related field.
  • 5+ years of experience in project management.
  • Proven experience in project management software tools, methodologies, and best practices.
  • Excellent leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills.
  • Strong organizational and multitasking abilities.
  • PMP or PRINCE2 certification is a plus.

    • Preferable

  • Experience in the IT industry is highly desirable.
  • Familiarity with Agile and Scrum methodologies.
  • Fluency in Arabic

Compensation & Benefits
